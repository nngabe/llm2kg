# Knowledge Graph Assistant

Welcome to the **Knowledge Graph Assistant**!

This application allows you to explore and query a knowledge graph built from various data sources.

## Features

- **Natural Language Queries**: Ask questions in plain English
- **Graph Visualization**: See the relevant knowledge graph context
- **Entity Disambiguation**: When names are ambiguous, I'll ask you to clarify
- **Step-by-Step Reasoning**: Watch the chain of thought as I process your query

## Example Questions

- "What do you know about [entity name]?"
- "How is [entity A] connected to [entity B]?"
- "Explain the relationship between [concept X] and [concept Y]"
- "What are the key facts about [topic]?"

## Tips

- Be specific with entity names when possible
- For complex queries, I'll show you the reasoning process
- Click on the graph visualization to interact with it
